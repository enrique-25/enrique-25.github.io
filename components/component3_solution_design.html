Design Constraints for Custom Vehicle

  • Weight Limit: The vehicle must weigh no more than 50 pounds to ensure it remains light enough for ease of use and transportation.
  • Maximum Speed: The vehicle’s speed must not exceed 5 mph for safety, given the user’s needs and condition.
  • Remote Control Range: The remote control must work at a minimum range of 50 feet to allow caregivers to operate the vehicle from a safe distance.
  • Battery Life: The vehicle must run for at least 3 continuous hours on a single charge, to ensure it can be used throughout various activities without frequent recharging.
  • Suspension: The suspension system must be able to handle terrain with inclines of up to 15 degrees, providing smooth operation across rough surfaces.
  • Lighting System: The vehicle must include at least 2 front-facing lights and 1 rear-facing light for safe nighttime use and visibility.
  • Seat Adjustment: The seat must have an adjustable range of 6 inches to accommodate Jorge’s growth over time.
  • Durability: The vehicle must withstand impacts from a drop of up to 2 feet without damage, ensuring its durability during use.
  • Material Safety: All materials used must be non-toxic and hypoallergenic, ensuring Jorge’s safety and comfort while using the vehicle.
  • User Interface Simplicity: The remote control must have no more than 4 buttons (on/off, forward, backward, stop), making it simple enough for caregivers to use without confusion.
