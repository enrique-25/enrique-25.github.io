Open-Back Mold Project

Safety Review

Key Safety Precautions:

  • Wear gloves and work in a well-ventilated area when handling silicone and modeling acrylic.
  • Avoid skin contact and wash immediately if exposed to prevent irritation.
  • Ensure proper curing time to prevent incomplete molds that may release fumes.

Reflection

Safety played a critical role in my design choices. By understanding material hazards, I ensured proper ventilation and wore protective gear. This influenced my mold design by focusing on smooth, easy-to-handle shapes that minimize unnecessary material exposure.
